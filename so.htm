Student Outcomes

A Apply knowledge of mathematics and science to solve complex manufacturing engineering problems;
  • Identify specific principles of mathematics, sciences, or engineering to solve complex engineering problems.
  • Apply appropriate models or equations to solve engineering problems.
B Design a system, component, or process to meet desired needs within realistic constraints, in accordance with standards;
  • Formulate criteria evaluation and analyze constraints of a design problem.
  • Design solution (prototype/system/component/process) that meets the relevant standards/constraints.
C Function in multidisciplinary and multi-cultural teams;
  • Participate, express ideas freely, and execute member tasks towards attainment of the objectives. (idea generation)
  • Integrate inputs from team members and make decisions based on objective criteria. (decision making)
D Design and conduct experiments, as well as to analyze and interpret data;
  • Design the experimental plan.
  • Collect sufficient and appropriate data to achieve the objectives of the experiment.
  • Analyze data using appropriate methods and draw out conclusions in accordance with engineering principles.
E Identify, formulate, and solve complex manufacturing engineering problems;
  • To specify the problem and identify the key issues, factors, and concepts affecting the problem.
  • Formulate and analyze various solutions to an engineering problem.
F Understand professional and ethical responsibility;
  • Interpret the ethical standards of the society/profession – case or exam.
  • Identify relevant professional codes and standards to design decisions.
G Communicate effectively;
  • Write grammatically correct and logical reports using appropriate graphics and technical style.
  • Present and defend ideas with sound reasoning and in an articulate manner.
H Understand the impact of manufacturing engineering solutions in a global, economic, environmental, and societal context;
  • Explain the impacts of the solution.
  • Establish the significance of the solution in terms of beneficiaries.
I Recognize the need for, and engage in life-long learning;
  • Reflect on the importance of acquiring new knowledge.
  • Critiquing journal papers/conduct literature review.
  • Establish the uniqueness of research based on previous studies.
J Know contemporary issues;
  • Identify contemporary issues in one’s field of specialization.
  • Determine the effect of contemporary issues in the discipline.
K Use techniques, skills, and modern engineering tools necessary for manufacturing engineering practice;
  • Select the necessary techniques or modern tools for a specific engineering task.
  • Demonstrate skills relevant to the practice.
L Know and understand engineering and management principles as a member and leader of a team, and to manage projects in a multidisciplinary environment;
  • Use appropriate tools and theories to manage multidisciplinary projects in a team.
  • Identify and devise solutions to real-world problems as a member/leader of a cross-functional team.
